A LITTLE ABOUT OUR AGENCY

Skyblue is the social network in which the student can participate in forums Video-lessons, enter libraries, share knowledge. This and more on one site. Our social network Allows Interaction between student-student and student-teacher, in which the Participants can ask questions, explain content, etc. On SkyBlue you teach and are taught. That book you I did not understand, it does not exist anymore.

Our mission is for you to learn In a spontaneous, fun and shared way. Let's go To promote a form of study so efficient that it will be impossible Do not study. There are several study sites with specific functionalities. Our Goal is to organize the functionality of these sites in one place, Promoting a broader range of data. While competitors focus on a maximum of 5 applications, We work so that several of these are used simultaneously.
